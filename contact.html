To join Auckland LUG...

To contact folks for the purposes of discussing Linux - tips, tricks, help requests, experiences and knowledge shared... this is the role of the Auckland LUG Mailing list. On the mailing list all manner of business relating to Linux and the User Group are discussed.

To join the mailing list visit the Google Groups AuckLUG page and follow the instructions for subscribing. (When the list archive pre December 2011 is back online, details on how to find it will be posted here.

If you have difficulties with the mailing list or have any enquiries about Auckland LUG events, your main point of contact is Glen Ogilvie. For the website, contact Mark Foster (hit up his Contact Page to drop him a note.

To contact the Administrators...

Monthly meetings are coordinated by Glen Ogilvie; email him using 'nelg' at linuxsolutions.co.nz.

Meetings & Events

AuckLUG hold monthly meetings, usually on the evening of the first weeknight of every month. These meetings are usually quite informal but often feature guest speakers on a Linux or Free/Open Source related subject, a meeting of minds and some light refreshments. Details of meetings can be found here (pre April 2012, link broken) or on our meetings page.

Not from Auckland?

Linux.net.nz maintain a list of Linux Users Groups from around NZ. There are User Groups in almost all parts of the country. If you can't find a group in your area, why not start one? If you're aware of a group not listed, please let Nic or Mark know.
